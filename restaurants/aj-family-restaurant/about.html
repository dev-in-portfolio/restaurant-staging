Our Story

Three generations of family recipes, one warm dining room.

From a family kitchen to Monroe's table

AJ Family Restaurant started the way most good things do — around a kitchen table. In 1998, Arthur and June Jenkins took the recipes June had spent decades perfecting and opened a small dining room on Main Street in Monroe. Their goal was simple: serve good food at fair prices and treat every guest like family.

Twenty-six years later, not much has changed. Arthur still starts the coffee at 5 AM. June still makes the biscuits by hand. Their son Michael runs the kitchen, and their granddaughter Sarah greets regulars by name at the front door.

We source from local farms whenever we can. Our chicken is fresh, our produce is seasonal, and our pies are baked from scratch each morning. Southern comfort food done right doesn't need shortcuts.

Whether you've been coming since 1998 or you're walking through our doors for the first time today — welcome. Pull up a chair.
